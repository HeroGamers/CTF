Master Baker's Brunsviger

Dough

  • 550g wheat flour
  • 60g sugar
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 60g butter
  • 50g yeast
  • 3 dl whole milk

Cream

  • ½ packet custard powder (Maizena)
  • 2 dl whole milk

Topping

  • 160g butter
  • 320g brown sugar

Instructions

Warm the milk and dissolve the yeast in it. Add salt, sugar, softened butter, and flour. Knead the dough well until smooth and slightly sticky. Sprinkle with a little flour and let it rise for about 30 minutes.

In a bowl, whisk 2 dl of milk with the custard powder for about 2 minutes until smooth.

Mix the softened butter with the brown sugar and fold in the custard cream.

Roll out the dough and place it in a baking tray. Spread the topping evenly over the dough and bake at 175°C for about 25 minutes.

Tips

  • Recommended baking tray size: approximately 38x44 cm (large oven tray).
  • Half a portion fits in a 25x35 cm tray.
